Calibration Services

We offer In-house and On-site calibration services for the pollution monitoring instruments. Our extensive instrument design knowledge enable us to offer the calibration services traceable to national standards. We utilise the latest technology, precise measurement techniques and vast experience of our trained engineers to deliver the most accurate results. We ensure that every instrument is calibrated and handled as per the documented standard procedures. We strongly believe in following the standard calibration procedures to ensure best results for customer needs.

  • Precise: Extensive instrument design knowledge and considerable test depth enable us to look ahead and identify deviations before they lead to problems.

  • Comprehensive: Our comprehensive manufacturer calibration includes preventative hardware maintenance, adjustments and modifications as well as regular updates. You can rest assured that your instruments are always state-of-the-art.

  • Traceable: The traceability of calibration results is a key requirement specified by standard organisations. The use of accredited calibrated working standards ensures direct traceability of measurement results to National Standards.

  • Quality assured: Our calibration procedures, quality standards and management systems provide assurance of quality for your instruments.

Instrument Training

We believe that detailed knowledge of instrument handling, enhance the Quality of Instrument itself. To enable our customers fully utilise our finest instruments, we offer the customer training via In-House training programmes. We also organise training at customer premises if required.

Servicing and Maintenance

So become very important to always keep your instruments in optimum condition. We offer servicing and maintenance services for wide range of instruments. We deliver the best results within short time. Our highly experienced engineers will keep you instruments ready for performance all around the year.
