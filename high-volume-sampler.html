High Volume Sampler VB HVS-101

Vayu Bodhak High Volume Sampler 101 is standard instrument to monitor Ambient Air Quality. It has wide use to measure air pollution in industrial areas, urban areas, on the shop floor, near monuments and other sensitive areas. It utilizes a calibrated orifice and high efficient filter paper to collect samples of air borne suspended particulate matter (SPM) which can be analyzed to get the concentration of SPM. It also has provision for simultaneous sampling of gaseous pollutants using the gaseous attachment. In this air sample is passed through suitable chemical reagents that absorbs the gaseous pollutants like SO2, NOx, Cl2 etc, which can be further analyzed by wet chemistry method to determine the concentration of gaseous pollutants.
