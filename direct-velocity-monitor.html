Direct Stack Velocity Monitor

Stack Monitoring involves a number of steps and calculations, which is time consuming and tedious. Specially when you are working in conditions like high temperature and have to carry heavy equipment. To solve all these we have designed the Direct Velocity Monitor. It helps you focus on monitoring without worrying about the complex formula calulations. It directly measures the values using sensors and displays the parameters on LCD.


SALIENT FEATURES

  • Scientifically designed case for easy handling during Stack Monitoring.
  • Rugged Design, Long operational life with smooth Functioning
  • Directly displays following parameters:
    * Stack Temperature
    * Stack Pressure
    * Stack Velocity
    * Flowrate for Nozzles of 1/8, 1/4 & 3/8.
    * Sampling Time for each Nozzle.
  • Calibrated S-type Pitot tube and Thermocouple provided in Kit
  • Easy Push-Fitting connections for Pitot and Thermocouple.
  • Backlight display for LCD for eay viewing in all conditions.
  • 1 Year Manufacturer Warranty.

Technical Specifications

  • Stack Temperature measurement range upto 600 degC.
  • Stack Pressure measurement range upto 200 mmH2O.
  • Stack Velocity measurement range upto 60 m/s.
  • Calculated flowrate for Nozzles of 1/8, 1/4 & 3/8.
  • Sampling time for each Nozzle calculted.
  • Calibrated S-Type Pitot tube of 0.6+0.6 Mtr.
  • K-Type Thermocouple of 0.6 Mtr with 2Mtr Cable.
  • Flexible PU pipe of 3Mtr x 2 for Pitot tube.
